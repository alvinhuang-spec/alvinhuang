The Movies

Deadpool (2016)

Release Year: 2016
Director: Tim Miller
Production Company: 20th Century Fox

Wade Wilson becomes Deadpool after an experiment leaves him with accelerated healing powers and a twisted sense of humor. He hunts down the man who nearly destroyed his life.

Deadpool 2 (2018)

Release Year: 2018
Director: David Leitch
Production Company: 20th Century Fox

Deadpool forms the X-Force to protect a young mutant from Cable, a time-traveling soldier. The film mixes outrageous comedy with heartfelt themes of family and sacrifice.
